Jiu Jitsu
As a side activity I enjoy practicing Jiu Jitsu. It's a fun and competitive sport that keeps me active and healthy. Jiu Jitsu has taught me humility and respect for others since, with proper technique, anyone can defeat another using Jiu Jitsu. The community is extremely supportive and it may seem competitive on the mat, but off the mat everyone is kind and helpful.
